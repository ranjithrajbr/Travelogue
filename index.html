Who we are

The word, “Travelogue” means ‘experiences of a traveler’ and we endower to showcase and represent these experiences that your brand offers and connect you with a captive audience in the Indian market space.

Our team with a collective experience of over 75 Years has the expertise in representing various travel & hospitality brands, destinations, and tourist attractions. There are a plethora of options that a buyer has to choose from and our aim is to bridge the gap between these opportunities with the buyers and sellers/promoters. We focus on innovative strategies, strong personal connections with a proactive approach. Backed by a strong IT & PR team, we take pride in our digital marketing, consultancy, and media services

“Our experience in the industry is being orchestrated to bridge the gap between your brand and the Indian market. With over 75 years of expertise, our knowledge is what makes us different from other companies as we represent brands in a way that makes them move closer to their goals.” - Jamsheed Hyder, Co-founder, Travelogue

Our Mission

We aspire to be the market leaders in the field of representation in India. We lead with professionalism, innovation, efficiency and diligently work towards the goals of our clients.

Our Vision

Why us

At Traveloguereps, we match every traveler to the best travel specialist. We have the right network of over 1,00,000 agencies in India that enables us to represent our clients to the larger audience in the Indian subcontinent. Having laid the foundation in Delhi, Bangalore, and Mumbai, we know where to market your product in India, and how to as well. Our 75 years experience has equipped us to set shop for your brand in India, thus helping you cut various miscellaneous expenses. At Travelogue, we believe that representation is about your brand and your goals as much as it is about our experience and quality delivery.
